Gym Tonic

Support

Gym Tonic is a workout app for creating and following gym workout cards.

Before Contacting Support

Try these steps first:

  • close and reopen the app;
  • check that notifications are enabled both inside Gym Tonic and in system settings;
  • export a manual backup before reinstalling or clearing app data;
  • confirm you are using the latest available version.

Common Questions

Does Gym Tonic require an account?

No. Gym Tonic works without accounts, authentication, cloud sync, or backend services in V1.

Where is my workout data stored?

Workout data is stored locally on your device.

Does Gym Tonic upload my workouts?

No. Gym Tonic does not upload workouts in V1.

How do backups work?

Gym Tonic can export a local JSON backup file. You choose where to save or share that file.

Backup files may contain workout notes and history, so keep them somewhere private.

Why did I not receive a rest notification?

Check that notifications are enabled in Gym Tonic settings, allowed in iOS or Android settings, and not suppressed by Focus or Do Not Disturb mode.
